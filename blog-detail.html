5 Ways Salons Can Use Brevo (It's More Than an Email Marketing Tool)

Maichi March 25, 2026 • 6 min read

Many salon owners think email marketing tools are only for sending newsletters. In reality, modern platforms have evolved into full business systems that help manage leads, automate communication and track bookings.

Platforms like Brevo combine CRM, automation, messaging and analytics into one place. Instead of juggling multiple tools for emails, client communication and lead tracking, salons can manage everything from one system.

For service businesses that rely on appointments and client relationships this can make a big difference.

Here are five ways salons can use Brevo to organize their marketing, streamline communication and turn more inquiries into booked appointments.

1. Capture and Organise Every Client Inquiry


Salons get inquiries from many different places Instagram DMs, WhatsApp messages, website forms and phone calls. When these conversations are scattered across different platforms its hard to track who inquired, what service they wanted and if they ever booked. .

Using the CRM inside Brevo salons can store every client contact in one place.

Each contact record can include:

  • • Name and contact information
  • • Service interest (hair, nails, skincare, bridal etc.)
  • • Appointment history
  • • Notes or tags from previous conversations

Once contacts are organised its much easier to track every potential client from their first inquiry to their final appointment.

Instead of searching through scattered messages salon staff can quickly see where each lead is at

2. Automate Follow-Ups With Potential Clients


One of the biggest reasons salons lose bookings is missed follow ups. A client inquires about a service, gets one response and then the conversation dies. Without reminders or additional messages many of these leads never convert into appointments. Automation solves this problem.

Brevo allows salons to create workflows where certain actions trigger messages. For example:

  • • A client submits an inquiry → they get an automatic confirmation message. • A consultation is booked → reminder emails or SMS messages are sent. • A potential client doesnt respond → the system sends a follow up message.

These automated messages keep the conversation active and reduce the risk of missed opportunities.

3. Send Targeted Email Campaigns and Promotions


Email marketing is still one of the most reliable ways to stay in touch with existing and potential clients.With Brevo salons can design and send professional email campaigns to promote services, announce updates or share seasonal offers.

Examples of email campaigns salons run include:

  • • Holiday promotions
  • • New service launches
  • • Loyalty rewards for repeat clients
  • • Special beauty packages or limited time discounts

Brevo also provides analytics such as open rates and click rates so salon owners can see which campaigns are getting the most interest. Over time this information helps refine marketing strategies and improve campaign performance.

4. Manage Client Conversations in One Inbox


Todays clients expect to communicate through multiple channels. A salon might get messages from Instagram, Facebook, SMS, website chat and email all in one day.

Switching between multiple apps can get overwhelming for staff members.

Brevo provides a unified inbox where conversations from different channels can be managed in one place. This allows salon teams to:

  • • View all messages from one dashboard • Respond to inquiries quickly • Track ongoing conversations with clients

When every conversation is logged and searchable its much easier to maintain consistent communication and deliver a better client experience.

5. Track Marketing Performance With Real Analytics


pMany small businesses rely on guesswork when it comes to their marketing. Without data its hard to know which campaigns are actually bringing in new clients.

Brevo provides analytics to help salons track:

  • • Email engagement rates
  • • Campaign performance
  • • Contact growth
  • • Client activity over time

These insights help salon owners know whats working and where to improve. Instead of making assumptions marketing decisions can be made with real data.

Turning Salon Inquiries Into a System

The most successful salons dont rely on scattered messages or manual follow ups. They build systems that capture inquiries, organise leads, automate communication and track performance. .

When these pieces work together the process is simple: Leads are captured → contacts are organised → automation follows up → conversations continue → analytics reveal what drives bookings.

This kind of system reduces missed opportunities and helps salons focus on what matters most: delivering great services to their clients.
